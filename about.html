About the wiki

This wiki runs on Wiki.js and is hosted in a VM on an Oracle Cloud server in Seoul. This wiki was created to provide a single location to store a variety of documentation covering D&D session notes, tutorials on using Foundry, setting up your own instances, other stuff. 

Why Wiki.js?

I chose Wiki.js as the foundation for this wiki since it shares the same runtime as Foundry, which is Node.js. Allowing me to apply similar approaches to hosting.

Can I contribute?

Yes! Currently, I'm the only contributor to this platform which I also manage. You can contribute by requesting an account here, or by sending me your notes! You can also suggest content you would like to have here such as tutorials.
