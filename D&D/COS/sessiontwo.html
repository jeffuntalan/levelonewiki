⇤Curse of Strahd

Summary

Day 1

  1. Arrival at the village of Barovia.
  2. PCs enter Blood on the Vine Tavern. Bangdrum chats with one of the Vistani owners, Ismark introduced himself later on and asks for help transporting Ireena to Vallaki.
  3. PCs accept quest, spends night in burgomaster's manor. Ireena wants to bury his deceased father first before leaving. PCs cast invisibility on Ireena, while Ram Ram and Mighty Tea cast disguise self on themselves, mimicking Ireena.

Day 2

  1. PCs pass by town square where Barovians fight over food from an old lady's cart.
  2. PCs bury the body in the church graveyard.
  3. Strahd introduces himself after the burial. Proclaims safety for Ireena, answers questions, and leaves.
  4. PCs leave Barovia, stumbles upon familiar faces in town square among the skirmishing Barovians.
  5. Bangdrum reunites with her brother, Mighty Tea reunites with Gallie who carries a bag of holding from Tresendar Keep.
  6. PCs start travel, reaching the Tser Pool Encampment by sundown.
  7. Madame Eva sees Ram Ram sneaking while other PCs drink and chat with Vistani, offers a Tarokka reading for the group.
  8. Tarokka Reading commences. Madame Eva answering a few questions and divulges her intentions.
  9. Madame Eva also manages to read the thoughts of PCs, gives Ram Ram and extra steed to prevent theft, and adds that Gallie has encountered a shapeshifting elk travelling away from Ravenloft. It is recalled that this was a previous challenger of Strahd's by the name of Mordenkainen.

Day 3

  1. PCs leave the encampment, stumbles upon a wandering trio of Revenants, which they defeat.

Date and Time at session end: 3rd day in Barovia (October 20th), 10 am.
