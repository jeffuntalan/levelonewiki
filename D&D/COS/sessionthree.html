⇤Curse of Strahd

Summary

Day 3

  1. PCs start to trek alongside the Tser River, reaching a high point where they needed to step out of the carriage and walk up the high road.
  2. PCs reach the bridge overlooking Tser Falls, continues travel. Rain starts pouring.
  3. Black Carriage at the crossroads looms along the misty road, horses get scared and stop. Volenta steps out and requests to see Ireena. Ram-Ram steals Strahd's Tarokka Deck from the coffin inside the carriage.
  4. Volenta declares that Ireena will be going with her, triggering an attack.
  5. Volenta solely takes on party, nears defeat, attacks Ireena instead. Strahd intervenes and forces Volenta back to the castle.
  6. As compensation, Strahd apologizes and hands PCs an invitation to dine at castle Ravenloft.
  7. PCs continue to Western Barovia, eventually reaching the gates of Barovia.
  8. PCs reach the outskirts of the mountains with horses exhausted. PCs risks exhaustion and goes to old mill.
  9. Ophelia answers PCs and allows them to rest in shed behind mill. Dream pastries were served, Tywin and Mighty Tea falls into a trance from eating them.

Day 4

  1. PCs wake up and are greeted by a business offer from Ophelia. She offers the players to sell their pastries in Vallaki, where they were headed.
  2. PCs talk to Morgantha about the proposition, eventually finding out the gruesome components needed to make the pastries.
  3. Battle ensues. PCs defeat the three hags: Morgantha (escaped), Ophelia (deceased), and Bella (deceased).
  4. Ireena wants to keep the remaining captured children, Lucien, Freek, and Myrtle. She opposes PCs' attempts to abandon them.
  5. PCs ingest three potions from the Old Mill, obtaining the effects of temporary youth, pale tincture, and cackle fever.
  6. PCs make their way to Vallaki, eventually bumping into Vasili von Holtz who helps them get past the wary nighttime guards.
  7. PCs bear witness to guards apprehending a Vallakian who was "not happy". PCs proceed to Arasek stockyard to find antidotes, but were closed out on when they tried to bargain. Ram-Ram does not go to stockyard, instead accompanies Vasili to drink.
  8. PCs make their way to the Blue Water Inn where they meet Urwin Martikov, the Innkeeper. PCs drink, chat, and eventually catches the eye of Rictavio, who stays at the Inn.
  9. Rictavio follows the players upstairs to the Inn's rooms and introduces himself as Rudolph van Richten, the vampire hunter. He asks about the Tarokka Ram-Ram holds and confirms its ties to Strahd.

[Session End]
