⇤Waterdeep: Dragon Heist

Opposite Invitations

          It was early evening at the Yawning Portal, and Birdflu has his eyes set on Yagra eating a sandwich by the well to Undermountain. He and his family had been tracking down the whereabouts of two Zhent agents, and they've found one of them. 

          The other members of the family started ordering some food while asking Bonnie some questions about the other Zhent agent, Davil Starsong. Bonnie told Flinch, Shrike, and Raven that Davil stayed at one of the rooms in the Yawning Portal, but hasn't been here today. She also mentioned that Davil and Yagra always meet here every night. Bonnie went back to the kitchen after taking their orders and answering a few questions. After a while, Yagra finished her sandwich and started walking to the bar. Birdflu followed.

          The bright lights in the Yawning Portal did not help Birdflu much with staying discreet, neither did his royal blue Harper cape. Yagra didn't notice him, but one person from the crowd did: Jalester Silvermane. Jalester approached Birdflu with the intent of inviting him to join the Lord's Alliance, failing to notice Birdflu's Harper Pin. He was immediately turned down by Birdflu, who was quite annoyed that he was getting bothered while observing Yagra. He referred his other family members to Jalester.

          Meanwhile, Bonnie returned to the Ultrævils' table with their orders and was immediately questioned once more by Flinch who was inquiring about Davil's likely whereabouts. Bonnie simple replied that she doesn't know, but they may ask someone who does. Before the Ultrævils could react, Bonnie called out for Yagra who seated by the bar. Everyone at the table was surprised at the sudden callout; Yagra approached the table.

          Bonnie explained to Yagra that the Ultrævils were looking for Davil. Yagra eyed them suspiciously, but decided that they didn't look threatening. She asked the seated adventurers why they were looking for Davil, to which they replied that one of them was interested in joining the Zhents. Yagra was surprised and started to talk in a hushed voice. She told the characters that Davil isn't available right now because he was preoccupied for matters she didn't want to discuss. She told the players to wait here at the Yawning Portal. With those words, Yagra bid them goodbye and returned to the bar.

          Jalester visited the table a few moments later, inviting the players over to join the Lord's Alliance. Flinch asked about the benefits of joining the faction. Jalester told them that members of the alliance each get a red-gold cape that is easily recognized by people (CHA advantage), as well as the ability to ask Waterdeep civilians and the city watch to assist you in times of need (combat). Members may also ask for a meeting with the Open Lord, should the need arise. Flinch expressed his desire to join, and told Jalester that he will be hearing from him soon. Meanwhile, Birdflu returned to the table and was informed by the the others about what Yagra told them. Robin was nowhere to be found the whole time.

          The family was now discussing the matter of what to report back to the Harpers. Birdflu felt that the task was still incomplete, and that they needed to locate Davil in order for the quest to be deemed complete. Flinch felt that knowing their common whereabouts was enough. The decision was settled via a vote, and ultimately the Ultrævils decided to track down Davil. They used up their remaining paper bird to write Davil's name, and used their horses to keep up with the paper bird. Flinch also used the light spell to make the paper bird easily discernible amidst the night sky.

Evening Eavesdropping

          Compared to the sewers where space was cramped and the paper bird flew in a limited space, keeping up with it in the open air proved to be a difficult task. Despite this, the Ultrævils managed to barely spot where it went. They were in the North Ward, that's for sure, and the place where the paper bird went was a walled off property just a few blocks away from Trollskull Manor.

          The Ultrævils heard laughter from the inside, and they assumed that they got the paper bird with the dirty note inside it. This further confirmed their suspicion that this was in fact, the Gralhund Villa. Birdflu knocked on the door of what seemed to be the garage of the villa. A thuggish figure opened the door, and inside they could see a carriage, and two other figure. These two other figures had their backs turned against them, exposing their neck tattoos in the shape of a flying snake. These people are Zhents.

          Birflu feigned asking for directions from the thug who opened the door. The thug nonchalantly gave directions to Birdflu's inquiry and immediately turned them down on any more inquiries, insisting that they should go. The doors slammed shut, and the Ultrævils heard nothing else but the crunching of the snow on the empty street as they tread along. As they approached the walls however, Raven heard voices coming from the other side.

          Curious about the conversation over the wall, the family decided to eavesdrop using a very comic method. Shrike boosted Flinch over the wall, and Flinch listened in on the conversation. Nearly 5 feet off the ground, Shrike and Flinch's teamwork allowed Flinch to peer over and see the figures engaged in conversation. Walking in the snow-trodden path was Yalah and Orond Gralhund, as well as Hrabbaz their bodyguard. A fourth figure was walking alongside them, listening to Yalah. This figure was a half-elf, whom they could only assume as Davil. Yalah seemed furious about something.

Yalah: "I don't care about what you're doing! Where is Urstul? Have him come by here immediately! That stone isn't gonna find itself!"

Orond: "Yalah, please calm down. I'm sure Urstul has been keen on finding the stone." Orond tells her.

Yalah: "Something is up. I just know it. Ahk, this long silence from Urstul is making me worry. I'm having wrinkles on my face, don't I Orond? Anyway, Davil I want you to find out what Mirt is up to. I noticed that he swapped with another actor in the middle of the performance last night. Get me information about what  that fat ass and his Harper crew is doing about the stone. I want eyes on them."

Davil: "Yes madame."

Yalah: "Orond, darling, please get me my purse I need to supply Davil with money for the mole."
Orond left along with Hrabbaz.

Yalah: "One more thing Davil, keep an eye on that goblin Ultrævil boy. You know the Ultrævils, correct? Those ridiculous excuses for nobles here in the North Ward. You see, one of them, the goblin boy, threatened me. I need you to dig up some dirt on them. Find out what they're here for. If they happen to be going for the stone as well, I want you to eliminate them. Ugh, no you can't possibly, if you can't,  have Urstul pay them a visit, that should be fun."

Orond: returns.

Yalah: "And here's your money, Davil. Go." Davil leaves the villa via the carriage house.

Yalah: "Orond, have the thing find the stone as well. These thugs are slow as slimes. My gut feeling tells me Dalakhar must have the stone by now. Give it the necklace as well. Okay?" She'll kiss Orond on the forehead.

Orond: "Don't worry dear, you'll have the money soon enough."

Yalah: "I know I will, one of these days I'll be a masked lord as well. Just you wait." She chuckles and they go inside the main house.
