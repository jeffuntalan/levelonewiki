The City of Splendors

Welcome! Our Waterdeep: Dragon Heist campaign will run for a maximum of 3 hours and 30 minutes each session, for about 8-12 sessions. This is a Tier 1+ adventure, and you can expect to be between the level 5-7 after the campaign ends.

Starting Season

  • Winter
    We shall begin our campaign in winter!

Waterdavian winters are harsh. As snow piles up around the city and ice fills the harbor, trade grinds to a halt and the city seals its gates. Citizens willing to brave the cold still gather in local taverns and festhalls, but few venture outside the city walls.

House Rules

"Bitch, I'm a dragon!" 

                      - a Kobold

1. Maximum Criticals

  • Bonus die for your nat20s are always maxed out.

2. Better Healing

  • Health potions will heal for their maximum amount. No dice roll needed. It still costs 1 action to use

                                                    or

  • You still roll for healing, but it now costs a Bonus Action

3. Lucky Inspiration

  • Inspiration points will be used the same way as the Lucky trait of halflings.

4. Stealth Rolls

  • Stealth rolls are done after you move your token just to remove any metagamey stealth hesitation on low rolls.

5. Funny Vicious Mockeries

  • Targets get no saving throw if the mockery makes anyone genuinely laugh. The bard deserved it!

6. Buddy Cop!

  • Two characters fighting back to back cannot be flanked, and they gain +2 AC. They must occupy the same square, so splash weapons and such are a bigger threat.

7. Intimidating?

  • Players get to choose if they want STR or CHA for intimidation.
